SummyBuddy

SummyBuddy is an upcoming and state of the art product that will accurately and efficiently summarize the contents of any body of text.

With tons of research papers and articles on the internet, most people don't have time to thoroughly peruse such texts. This is why our team of four UCSD ECE 140B students decided to create SummyBuddy to help with this issue.
